Electric Valve Actuators

Print

Overview

Available in multi-turn, part-turn, linear and lever configurations, Paladon Systems' comprehensive range of electric valve actuators are suitable for cost effective and reliable automation of virtually any valve size and type.

Operating Ranges

Power Supply

  • Suitable for all common power supply standards between 24 VDC and 690 VAC operation (reliable operation even at -30% of expected mains voltage)

Torque Output

  • Between 10 Nm and 675,000 Nm

Key Features & Benefits

  • Modular design allows for flexible operation and easy upgrades
  • Controls can be integrally or remotely mounted
  • High output torque for automation of high pressure valves
  • Absolute reliability and safeguards against failure
  • Redundancy concepts of applied Distributed Control Systems are wholly supported
  • Fieldbus technology and lifecycle management
