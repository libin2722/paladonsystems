   January 2016, Issue 21

Dear Visitor,

Well, the New Year has begun but we are left with some of the problems from the old year. Business is tough, the oil price has fallen further than the most pessimistic forecasts from 2015 and now some are predicting prices as low as $10/bbl!
 
Moving on, the news since our last communication is generally positive. See below for updates regarding our progress with customer approvals and our first order from PDO in Oman. The market reaction to our new technology developments has been very positive and, since ADIPEC last November, we have secured several orders for our Self-Contained Electro-Hydraulic solar powered systems.
 
The Oil Industry is not out of the woods yet. This year will be particularly challenging as the Oil Industry is forced to adapt to the new financial climate, but hopefully with its move towards newer technologies, Paladon Systems will be suitably positioned with our safe, reliable and environmentally friendly valve automation solutions.
 
My sincere thanks for all your support in 2015 and we look forward to supporting you in 2016.  
 
Brian Ennever
Petroleum Development Oman (PDO) Approval
Paladon Systems is delighted to announce that their quarter-turn and linear actuators and control systems have been approved for on/off and positioning applications by PDO. Paladon Systems Marketing Manager, Matthew Shepherd, “PDO’s approval process was the longest and strictest that I’ve ever experienced in my 20 plus years in this industry and their approval is a testament to the dedication and professionalism of the entire team at Paladon Italia. My sincere thanks to everyone at Paladon Italia and to our Omani representative (Gemini Energy) for all their support during the demanding 13 month approval process”. Find out more about PDO and download the approval certificate.
First Order Received for a PDO Project
After recently receiving PDO approval, Paladon Systems is pleased to confirm that they have received their first order for the Dhahaban South Well project. To be fitted to a 6” Class 900 choke valve, Paladon Systems will be supplying a ring-main powered positioning and ESD system. EExd certified, the system will provide precise valve positioning using Foundation Fieldbus communications and fail the valve closed on loss of either the command signal or hydraulic supply pressure. Find out more about valve positioning systems.
Power Valves & Controls Joins Paladon Systems’ Business Partner Network
Founded in 2011, Power Valves & Controls specialises in supplying high quality valves and accessories, primarily to traditional power stations located in Benelux. Power Valves & Controls Managing Director, Gerrit De Baene,”We are thrilled with our partnership with Paladon Systems. We believe there is a huge potential in the European market for their controllable and often custom made electro-hydraulic systems.  Furthermore, what impresses us is Paladon Systems’ drive to include new technologies, such as solar panels, low power requirements and Bluetooth communications in their designs”. Find out more about Power Valves & Controls and Turbine Bypass Systems.
Abu Dhabi Oil Refining Company (TAKREER) Approval
Paladon Systems is pleased to announce that they have now been approved by TAKREER for the supply of fuel gas packages, actuator control systems and electro-hydraulic systems. TAKREER was established in 1999 to take over the responsibilities of refining operations from Abu Dhabi National Oil Company (ADNOC). TAKREER’s operations currently cover crude oil and condensate refining, supply of petroleum products and the production of granulated sulphur. Find out more about TAKREER.
Autonomous Shutdown Valve (ASV) Product Video
The Autonomous Shutdown Valve (ASV) has been specifically designed for the operation of subsea PLEM valves used in CALM buoy offshore terminals. It's pioneering technology provides a step change in terms of reliability and environmental protection when compared to all traditional methods of subsea PLEM valve operation. In addition to numerous operational benefits, the ASV also saves Operators significant OPEX when compared to all traditional technologies. Watch the 3 minute video to find out what makes the ASV the obvious choice for CALM buoy terminal operators.

Abu Dhabi Marine Operating Company Approval
Abu Dhabi Marine Operating Company (ADMA-OPCO) has recently approved Paladon Systems’ rotary and linear pneumatic and hydraulic actuators, electro-hydraulic and gas-over-oil systems; as well as all other types of actuator control systems. Paladon Systems Sales Manager – Projects, Lynette Ross; “ADMA-OPCO is one of the major offshore oil and gas producers in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi. This latest approval is a key part of our strategy to grow our market share and bring our pioneering technology to this extremely active region”. Find out more about ADMA-OPCO.
