North East Bab 3 Project Win
Having successfully supplied choke valve positional control systems for an earlier phase of ADCO’s North East Bab 3 (NEB-3) project, Paladon Systems is pleased to announce that they have now been awarded the order to supply the control valve positioning systems for this prestigious project. The order comprises 142 ring-main positioning systems powered by a 185 to 200 Barg external hydraulic supply and will provide a fail-closed failure mode on loss of either the hydraulic supply or 4-20mA HART command signal. Additionally, the order includes 12 self-contained electro-hydraulic positioning systems powered by a 24VDC supply and will provide a fail-open failure mode on loss of the electrical supply or command signal. Find out more about electro-hydraulic valve positioning systems.

Iraqi Project Win
GE Oil & Gas has placed an order for the Aumara Depot Pressure Reduction project. To be fitted to 24” Class 600 globe valves, Paladon Systems will be supplying 3 self-contained electro-hydraulic valve positioning systems. These EExd certified systems will provide precise valve positioning with an accuracy of ±0.5% as well as a fail-safe position on loss of either the 380V electrical supply or 4-20mA command signal. Paladon Systems Sales Engineer, Wojtek Nowakowski, “We have previously worked closely with GE Oil & Gas on several large power generation projects and are delighted that our successful completion of these technically demanding projects has helped secure this latest order.” Find out more about GE Oil & Gas and self-contained electro-hydraulic systems.

Latest Customer Approval
In 2005 ADCO approved Paladon Systems’ electro-hydraulic systems, followed by their pneumatic and hydraulic actuators in 2014. After a rigorous application process, Paladon Systems is pleased to announce that ADCO has now also approved their turnkey HIPPS packages and ESD systems. The Abu Dhabi Company for Onshore Petroleum Operations Ltd. (ADCO) operates onshore and in shallow coastal water of the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, one of the seven members of the United Arab Emirates which was established on 2nd December 1971. Find out more about ADCO and HIPPS.

Asia Pacific Quality Assurance
Paladon Systems Sdn. Bhd. has been awarded ISO 9001 certification. Paladon Systems' Vice President Sales – Asia Pacific Region, Anba Nagappan, “Quality was one of Paladon Systems’ founding principles and still continues to drive everything we do today. It is worth noting that our UK headquarters was first awarded ISO 9001 certification back in 1989 and, in fact, was also one of the first companies in the energy industry to receive it. Since opening late in 2013, our Malaysian facility has been working hard towards achieving ISO 9001 certification. We are proud to announce to our Asia Pacific clients that they can now have the same quality assurance that they have enjoyed from our UK and Italian facilities, but with the extremely short deliveries and rapid service support that only a local Paladon Systems facility can provide." Fraud Alert
Please note that a company calling itself, "Paladon Oil and Gas Company UK (POG)" is sending out fraudulent offers for employment at Paladon Systems. "Paladon Oil and Gas Company UK (POG)" is not related to Paladon Systems in any manner and unfortunately these fraudulent job offers have already resulted in several people being conned out of several thousand pounds. Should you receive a job offer from, "Paladon Oil and Gas Company UK (POG)" for employment at Paladon Systems, we recommend that you contact your local trading standards organisation to report the fraud.
