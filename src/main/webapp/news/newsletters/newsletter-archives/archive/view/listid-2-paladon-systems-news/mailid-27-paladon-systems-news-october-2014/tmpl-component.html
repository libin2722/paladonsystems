  Paladon Americas' Solar Solution
A major American oil company needed to better control an oil pipeline located in a remote region of the Permian Basin, but faced two major challenges. 1) The existing manual gate valves could not be operated during periods of heavy rainfall as the site becomes unreachable due to flooding. 2) No external electrical, pneumatic or hydraulic supply sources were available to power valve actuators. Paladon Americas solved these challenges through the design and supply of four solar-powered self-contained electro-hydraulic linear actuator systems which were retrofitted to the existing 4” Class 600 gate valves. In addition to providing 24/7/365 remote wireless valve control, the systems were fitted with automatic valve partial stroke test systems to provide condition monitoring and allow the end user to implement a cost-saving preventive maintenance program. Find out more about self-contained electro-hydraulic and valve partial stroke test systems.

CCOE Approval Award
Paladon Systems is pleased to announce that they have been awarded by PESO (Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organisation) CCOE (Chief Controller of Explosives) approval to supply valve on/off and positioning electro-hydraulic systems into the Indian market for use in hazardous area locations. Formerly known as the Department of Explosives, PESO is headquartered in Nagpur and was founded on 9th September 1898. Find out more about PESO and download the approval certificate.
World's Deepest Offshore Gas Wells Project Win
Paladon Systems has recently been awarded an order for the supply of 8 smart electro-hydraulic linear positioning systems. The systems will be fitted to high pressure choke valves used to provide precise 2 stage pressure regulation of an upstream well pressure of 30,000 psig. Product Manager Mike Northwood, “This application is about as extreme as it gets and one that challenges even our advanced valve positioning technology. We have been involved with the development of this particular gas field since 2011 and this latest substantial order is a testament to the ruggedness and reliability of our systems as well as our customer’s confidence in Paladon Systems’ ability to deliver." Find out more about electro-hydraulic positioning systems.
