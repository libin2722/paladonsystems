Songket Petroleum was established with the goal to provide our customers with the best technology, equipment solutions and services in the Oil and Gas, Petrochemical, Refinery, Mining and Energy industries. Songket engages in the manufacturing, procurement, modification, installation and services of valve actuation products.

We offer finest quality range of tailored ball valves, valve actuators and control systems for all sectors particularly the oil and gas and energy industry. Our Principles are established manufacturers having wealth of experience and expertise and are well known for their high quality precision engineering products.
